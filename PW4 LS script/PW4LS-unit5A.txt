Back then, fear motivated us to deal with them. It\

Another problem caused by fear is post-traumatic\

Phobias and PTSD can be treated by trained therapists\
using exposure therapy. In this type of treatment,\
the cause of the fear is shown to the person in gradual\

Hauner did an experiment with 12 adults who had a\

Hauner scanned the brain of each of the 12 adults as\

Building on this success, Hauner did another\
experiment to weaken fear memories during sleep.\
This time, she worked with a group of healthy adults\
without phobias or PTSD. The first step was to place a\
fear in their brains. For this, she used a process called\

like mint or lemon. The people would also link this\

When they looked at the photos, the fear response\
